Hi! My name is Zoe Del Rosario.

I love to code and design things. I live in the Bay Area and am currently a student at Campolindo High School
and will be graduating in 2018. I am a big advocator of learning through my mistakes and listening to others.

I live by these words from a personal idol, Tyler Joseph:
Do not give up. Push through the droughts. Channel the inevitable disappointments into your craft.
Break molds. Think. Create. And most importantly, stay alive. And in the meantime, make it about others.
That seems to work. Stay strong. Live on. Power to the local dreamer.

Aside from coding and school, I love creating, baking, and music. I enjoy creating art in all different mediums, from acrylic
paints to pen and ink. When I am not sketching in my sketchbook, you can find me tackling various recipes, usually aided by my mother.
Furthermore, I live and breathe music. Through perseverance and concentration, I have taught myself how to read sheet music and
play the piano and ukulele. I enjoy playing pieces from the my favorite bands and I like playing music with friends.
